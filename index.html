Cidalia Eusebio

We all have a purpose – something that drives us. Like the forces that press us into our seats, but also propel us forward, when an aircraft takes off. It is the unstable path – the risk that makes you feel worthy. And nothing makes me more motivated than a: wicked healthcare problem. Helping governments, hospitals, and services strengthen complex healthcare systems, to achieve better outcomes for all, through evidence, public health, and innovation. Cidalia offers contributions to understanding the complexities of health systems. Through collaboration with multidisciplinary teams, she helps transform, create, and innovate, striving for a healthcare service that is better for everyone. Cidalia consults privately. Currently, she is participating in the European Health Parliament collaborating to set recommendations for a Future Proofing Health System. Cidalia has a master’s degree in Public Health, with specialization in Health Services Management, from the London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine, and attended Harvard Medical School and MIT, studying healthcare innovation and social entrepreneurship. From a clinical perspective, Cidalia’s background is as a critical care nurse specialist, mainly in cardiothoracic and ECMO. She has also worked in a variety of acute clinical settings, as well in Public Health (Health Protection). Cidalia is just one piece of a very complex puzzle – she clinically consults on healthcare and public health problems, identifying new solutions through change..
